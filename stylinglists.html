Unordered-List bullets can be changed from disks to many other values.
ul{ list-style-type: X }; where X can be:
1.circle : Rings
2.square : Black Squares
3.decimal : Number(Now it will be an ordered list)
4.lower-greek: Uncapitalised greek symbols
5.katakana : japanese symbols
For making images as your bullet types: ul{ list-style-image: url(url of the image here)};
